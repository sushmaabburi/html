potato fry

potatoes are one of my favourite foods. Here is the recipe for delicious potato fry

Ingredients

chilly powder
A powder made of chilles(required)
  • 4 potatoes
  • oil as needed
  • salt needed
  • garam masala

Method

  1. To stir fry always use potatoes that are not from the frozen section as they don't get cooked or roasted quickly.
  2. Always keep the potatoes immersed in a bowl of water to get rid of the excess starch.
  3. use garam masala to spice up the aloo fry.
  4. Always add salt to potatoes only after they are cooked well.
